## Performance & Interdisciplinary

<div class="culture-grid">

<div class="culture-card">
<div class="culture-thumb-placeholder">🎭</div>
<div class="culture-body">
<h3><a href="/culture/crowd-walk/">Crowd Walk (2019)</a></h3>
<span class="culture-tag">Performance</span><span class="culture-tag">BCI</span><span class="culture-tag">Ilmin Museum</span>
<p class="culture-desc">Performed with artist <strong>Lang Lee</strong> at <em>Ilmin Museum of Art</em>, Seoul. Real-time emotion recognition from facial expression and EEG drove a crowd-movement performance piece — the audience's collective affective state became a choreographic parameter.</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="culture-card">
<div class="culture-thumb-placeholder">🎬</div>
<div class="culture-body">
<h3><a href="/culture/doona-bae/">Project Doona Bae (2021)</a></h3>
<span class="culture-tag">Neuroscience</span><span class="culture-tag">Book chapter</span>
<p class="culture-desc">Behavioral and fMRI study of actress Doona Bae's empathic abilities, published as a chapter in the companion book <em>Actress Doona Bae</em>. Examined neural correlates of screen performance and empathic resonance using quantitative imaging methods.</p>
</div>
