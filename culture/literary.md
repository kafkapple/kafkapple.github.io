**Award**: Honorable Mention.
**Contest**: 22nd KAIST Literary Award.

KAIST's Literary Award is an annual contest open to KAIST community
members across categories including poetry, short story, and essay/
criticism. The contest emphasizes literary quality alongside intellectual
engagement.
