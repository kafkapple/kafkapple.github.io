**Role**: Guitar.
**Genre**: Metalcore.
**Origin**: Daejeon, Republic of Korea.

Noeazy (노이지) is a Korean metalcore band formed in the mid-2000s,
known for international competition success and a sustained underground
following.

## EBS Space Sympathy (2012)

Broadcast performance on EBS, a Korean public TV music program.

{% include youtube.html id="Ok0LP__lWIg" title="Noeazy — EBS Space Sympathy 2012" quality="maxresdefault" %}

## Emergenza International Final, Germany (2016)

Won 3rd award in the international final round of the Emergenza Festival,
held annually at the Taubertal Festival in Germany. Competed against
finalists from across Europe and Asia.

{% include youtube.html id="UnGYD1y9-wg" title="Noeazy — Emergenza International Final, Germany 2016" quality="maxresdefault" %}

## Highlights

- **2016** — 3rd award, Emergenza International Final, Germany
- **2012** — EBS Space Sympathy broadcast performance
- Recorded EPs and toured Korea / select international venues
