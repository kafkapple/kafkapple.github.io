**Role**: Guitar.
**Genre**: Metalcore.
**Origin**: Daejeon, Republic of Korea.

Noeazy (노이지) is a Korean metalcore band formed in the mid-2000s,
known for international competition success and a sustained underground
following.

## Taubertal Festival 2016 — Emergenza International Final (Germany)

3rd award at the **Emergenza Festival International Final**, held annually
at the **Taubertal Festival** in Germany. Competed against finalists from
across Europe and Asia.

## Highlights

- **2016** — 3rd award, Emergenza International Final, Germany (Taubertal Festival).

- Multiple EP releases and tours of Korea + select international venues.
