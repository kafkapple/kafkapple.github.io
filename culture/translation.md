**Book**: ***A Thousand Brains: A New Theory of Intelligence*** by **Jeff Hawkins** (2021).

## About

I served as a domain reviewer for the Korean translation of Jeff Hawkins'
*A Thousand Brains*, which proposes that intelligence emerges from
thousands of cortical columns each building reference frames of the world.
The book is targeted at general readers but draws on cortical
neuroscience and predictive coding — a domain where Korean scientific
translation often introduces drift if reviewers aren't careful.

My role: cross-check terminology (cortical columns, voting, reference
frames) for fidelity to the original meaning while preserving readability.

## Notes

A useful reminder that *the same concept* in English and Korean may
trigger very different default associations — "reference frame" in
English implies coordinate-system precision; the most natural Korean
rendering can read more like "framework". Translation reviews force
exactly this kind of conceptual disambiguation.
