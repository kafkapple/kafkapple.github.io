**Year**: 2021.
**Role**: Project leader.
**Subject**: Actress **Bae Doona (배두나)**.
**Methods**: Behavioral assays + fMRI (KAIST Brain Dynamics Laboratory).

## Project

A neuroscience-meets-art collaboration assessing empathic and observational
abilities of a working actress. Behavioral tests + fMRI scanning were
designed to probe whether trained acting practice correlates with
neural activation patterns associated with theory-of-mind and emotional
inference.

## Output

The findings appear as a chapter ("Doona Bae's brain") in the book
*Actress Doona Bae* — a hybrid work combining art-criticism and
neuroscience, written for general readers.

## Notes

This project sits at an unusual intersection: rigorous neuroscience methods
applied to a single high-engagement subject (a case study), framed for a
non-academic audience. It taught me a great deal about how to *write*
about neuroscience without losing precision.
